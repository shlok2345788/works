Hindustan Unilever Mixed Reality Maintenance
We have transformed and improved practices at the Hindustan Unilever with Change Over Parts and Autonomous Maintenance using Mixed Reality, at the Rhino Plant at the NESA Plant,
Doom Dooma, Assam. Effective and efficient maintenance processes are important elements that support the production control process for most of the manufacture and maintenance process. This shortens the production
lead times, helps in achieving higher quality standards. The most significant positive impact of having a small changeover time is the batch size reduction in the production system. The added benefits of the short changeover
time includes the expenses reduction, faster production speed, increased output, lead times reduction, smoother flows, a broader range of lot sizes, lower inventory and increased customer satisfaction.
