Hindustan Unilever Mixed Reality Maintenance

We have transformed and improved practices at the Hindustan Unilever with Change Over Parts and Autonomous Maintenance using Mixed Reality, at the Rhino Plant at the NESA Plant, Doom Dooma, Assam. Effective and efficient maintenance processes are important elements that support the production control process for most of the manufacture and maintenance process. This shortens the production lead times, helps in achieving higher quality standards. The most significant positive impact of having a small changeover time is the batch size reduction in the production system. The added benefits of the short changeover time includes the expenses reduction, faster production speed, increased output, lead times reduction, smoother flows, a broader range of lot sizes, lower inventory and increased customer satisfaction.

ISRO SAC Planet Terrain VR

A VR tool to input any satellite elevation model of any resolution to generate 3D terrain with textures and vector data in real-time to collaborate and analyse the planetary data. The software uses our algorithms based on months of research to generate the terrain and teleport the user at the right location in VR. The tool currently features measuring the distance between two findings, textual & voice annotations, altitude measurements & VR 2D capture.
